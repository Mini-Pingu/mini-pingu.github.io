Here is an interesting story of my ex-colleague: 

His name is Mr. F. At first, he was hired as a sales man, who in change of promoting the products of the company. As time goes by, the unrelated workload are pushing on his shoulders: prepare material of upcoming presentation , maintain the outdoor machines, etc. He was getting exhausting about those unassociated small tasks. 

He noticed I had been working on a silly React Project for a while at that time. One day, he could not help but ask me why I was staring all day with the black screen. I told him I was working on a website, with a well paid. Suddenly, I gained his interests as he showed the powerful sheer will of learning IT skills, especially website decoration. 

I just repeated what I had learned from the Internet to him, the path for all beginners is starting from HTML, CSS and ended with Javascript. He was really diving in for several weeks, honestly. However, he could not stick to it **all the way**. Too many factors was interfering him, mostly debts. Eventually, I do not understand why He quited.
