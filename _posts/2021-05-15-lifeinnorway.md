#### Notes from lifeinnorway.net

According to my knowledge, the requirement of gaining a resident permit in Norway is living there with a job at least three years. The main point here: how to get a jobs in Norway as an newcomer. 

I am going to make a deep reading of this site, section by section, and I will make a summary of each section.

#### Books

The most important part of this section is [How to Find a Job in Norway](https://www.lifeinnorway.net/books/how-to-find-a-job-in-norway/). This book is selling on Amazon, and some other  e-market. However, I cannot search the free download on the Internet, and my credit card is not ready yet, so I will take a step back and check out the rest of this site first.

#### Podcast

The editor, David Nikel, knows that not everyone loves reading, so that he start the serious of podcast channel to introduce Norway. These podcasts are mostly letting use know more about the culture, or so-called "soft power" of Norway. Each episode is around forty minutes remain.
